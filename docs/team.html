About The Team

The team members for this consisted of Jordan James, Shaun Massenburg, Jaxon Abele, and Zachery Schleger who all contributed particular skills to help pull this project together. Over the course of the 2023 Spring Semester, the team constructed this webpage and all data on it for their final project in the large scale text analysis class at Penn State Behrend.
